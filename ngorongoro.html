Why Ngorongoro Crater?

The Ngorongoro Crater is 600m deep and 20km wide. Over 25,000 animals live inside including the endangered black rhino. Because animals can't leave the crater, game viewing is guaranteed year-round. No other place in Africa lets you see lions, elephants, buffalo, leopards, and rhino in 6 hours.

Big 5 Guaranteed

Highest chance to see all Big 5 in one day. Especially black rhino.

Stunning Views

The view from the crater rim at sunrise is unmatched. Perfect for photos.

Maasai Culture

Visit a real Maasai village on the way. Learn about their traditions.

What to Expect on Your Ngorongoro Tour

5:30 AM: Early pickup from your Karatu lodge. Descend into crater as gates open.

6:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Game drive on crater floor. Spot lions, hyenas, hippos, flamingos at Lake Magadi, and rare black rhino.

12:00 PM: Picnic lunch at Ngoitokitok picnic site near hippo pool.

1:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Final game drive as we ascend out of crater.

Note: Only 6 hours allowed inside crater by TANAPA rules. We maximize every minute.
